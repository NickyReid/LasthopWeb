About
Explore your musical journey with Lasthop. Simply input your last.fm username to see the artists and songs you enjoyed on this day in previous years.

Soon, you'll be able to generate a Spotify playlist with the highlights of your Lasthop results.

To get early access to the Spotify playlist feature, send a request to lasthopweb@gmail.com with your last.fm username and the email address you use to log in to Spotify.

This is an Open Source project. You can view it, fork it, or submit an issue on Github.
